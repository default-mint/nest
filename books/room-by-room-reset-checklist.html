About this ebook

A whole home can feel impossible to tackle, so this checklist breaks it into rooms and rooms into clear steps. Each chapter walks one space from cluttered to calm with a repeatable sequence you can follow without thinking. You decide the order, work one room at a time, and never face the entire house at once. Structure is what turns an overwhelming job into a series of doable resets.

Beyond the first deep reset, the book gives each room a short maintenance routine so the work holds. The approach stays practical throughout. No special products, no rigid rules, just a sensible order of operations for each space and a clear idea of what done looks like. Follow it room by room and your whole home steadily comes back into order.
